How do I sell tickets on TycketHub?

Log on to your TycketHub account

Find the ticket (or tickets) you no longer need.

Hit ‘Resell’.

Set the price. This will be the same price you paid, or less if you wish.

That’s it, your tickets will be listed on TycketHub. We’ll automatically reissue most tickets to the buyer. You’ll get your money back and another fan gets to go to the show.
